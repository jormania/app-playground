Codex Alchymicus — KCD2
28 recipes · 6 categories · parchment UI
gaming alchemy KCD2 offline-ready

A parchment-styled recipe manager for Kingdom Come: Deliverance II alchemy. All 28 learnable recipes pre-loaded, searchable by name and ingredient, filterable by category. Track what you've mastered, add custom brews.

  • Animated SVG candle flames and layered parchment textures
  • Hand-drawn herb icons: nettle, belladonna, marigold, mint, valerian…
  • Category filtering, difficulty pips, mastery toggle, recipe deletion
  • Custom recipe input modal with ingredient + step parsing
  • Mobile layout with panel-switching navigation

Kettlebell Training
12 exercises · Keith Haring style · mobile-first
fitness training SVG mobile-optimised

12 essential kettlebell exercises — rated by difficulty, illustrated with vectorial Keith Haring-style figures. Sticky nav, 6 steps per exercise, and a running commentary that takes itself exactly as seriously as it should.

  • Swing, Turkish Get-Up, Goblet Squat, Clean & Press, Windmill and more
  • 1–3 strongman difficulty icons per exercise (Haring style)
  • Reusable SVG symbol library — 6 figures, 0 image requests
  • Sticky nav with IntersectionObserver active-link tracking
  • Optimised for Samsung S24 (412 px Android viewport)
