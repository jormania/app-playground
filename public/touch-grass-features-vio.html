Touch Grass · Thrive v1
// guide

A mindful walking companion.

Touch Grass · Thrive is a warm, personal version of the original — built around presence, self-care, and gentle noticing. Step outside without a goal, walk for however long you need, and come back to see what the walk quietly left behind.

// how to use

  1. Tap ↺ to find a green space nearby, or type where you're going.
  2. Tap Step outside — your departure saves immediately. Close the app, lock your screen, just walk.
  3. Come back when you're ready. There's no minimum or maximum time.
  4. Tap I'm back and see what the walk left behind.
finds

Mindful discoveries

Every walk returns a small set of quiet gifts — sensory moments, fleeting insights, and gentle noticing. Never advice, never heavy. Finds are intentionally few: a short walk brings one, an hour brings two or three. Each is generated to be genuinely distinct in kind and register.

Four depths of presence:

  • common A simple sensory noticing. Something already there that needed seeing.
  • uncommon A small shift. A thought that arrives gently and doesn't insist.
  • rare A moment of genuine release or unexpected clarity.
  • legendary A profound sense of arriving, belonging, or being completely present.
location

Nearby places

Tap ↺ and the app suggests a real green space near you — parks, forests, gardens, nature reserves. The list is pulled from your GPS each session. You can also type any location, or leave it blank and just walk.

audio

Ambient soundscape

While you're outside, the app plays a soft generative ambient mix — wind, birdsong, rain, or crickets, depending on where you said you're going, the time of day, and the live weather. Toggle with the speaker icon on either the welcome or away screen.

weather

Live weather

Current conditions are fetched automatically from your location. Rain adds streaks across the sky. Storm deepens the wind. The ambient sound follows. No API key needed for this.

away

Away screen

Once you step outside, the app shows a minimal screen that gently sways — like a sign left on a door. Your location and departure time are visible. Feel free to close the tab or lock your screen; the timer persists until you return.

sky

Seasonal canvas

The sky shows a sun or moon based on the time, drawn with the actual current lunar phase. The grass shifts warmer through the seasons — meadow-green in spring and summer, golden in autumn, pale in winter. Weather adds rain streaks, snow, or fog.

stats

Streak, walks & collection

Three boxes track your practice at a glance. All three dim when empty and brighten as you go.

  • 🔥 Streak — tap to see your current streak, personal best, and total days walked.
  • 👣 Walks — tap to see time outside by week, month, year, and all time. Includes your longest walk.
  • 🎁 Found — tap to browse everything collected from your walks, sorted by depth of presence.
setup

API key

Tap ⚙ to add your Anthropic API key. It stays in your browser and is never shared. A small credit goes a long way — a few dollars covers hundreds of walks. The app uses Claude to generate your finds.

install

Install on Android

Open in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu, choose Add to Home Screen, then Install app. It launches full-screen with no browser bar, like a native app. Works with Minimalist Phone and similar launchers.
