Touch Grass v7
// feature guide

Everything Touch Grass does.

A complete reference for the app. The app itself is intentionally minimal — this page is where the details live.

// how to use

  1. Tap ↺ to pick a nearby green space, or type any location.
  2. Tap Head outside and go. The timer saves immediately — close the tab, lock your screen, it doesn't matter.
  3. Come back, open the app, tap I'm back.
  4. See what you found.
location

Nearby places

Tap ↺ and the app suggests a real green space near you — parks, forests, gardens, nature reserves. The list is pulled fresh from your GPS each session. If nothing's found nearby, you can always type a location manually.

finds

Surreal discoveries

Every walk generates a small set of impossible things you might have found — dreamlike, poetic, never realistic litter. Finds are intentionally scarce: a 20-minute walk returns one, an hour returns three. Each one is generated to be genuinely different from the others — different sensory register, scale, and strangeness. Four tiers:

  • common Mildly uncanny. Something real that feels slightly wrong.
  • uncommon Clearly impossible. Physical laws politely ignored.
  • rare Reality-bending. The ground doesn't behave as expected.
  • legendary Mythic. Awe-inspiring. Rare enough to feel like an event.

A solo short walk always returns at least an uncommon find. Legendaries are approximately 1-in-100.

audio

Ambient soundscape

While you're outside, the app plays a generative ambient mix that adapts to where you said you're going, the time of day, and the weather. Birds in parks during daylight. Crickets at night. Rain when it's raining. The sound toggle is available on both the welcome screen and the away screen — tap the speaker icon any time to mute or unmute.

away

Away screen

Once you step outside, the app shows a minimal "away" screen that gently sways like a sign left on a door. Your departure location and time are displayed. Feel free to close the tab or lock your screen — the timer persists. Tap I'm back when you return.

weather

Live weather

The app fetches current conditions from your location on load. Rain adds streaks to the canvas. Storm intensifies the wind. Fog softens everything. The ambient sound mix follows along. No API key needed for this.

sky

Celestial body

The sky shows a sun or moon based on the time — never both. The sun shifts warm at dawn and dusk. The moon reflects the actual current lunar phase. Clouds pass in front depending on conditions.

canvas

Seasonal grass

The grass colour shifts with the calendar. Vivid green in spring, lush in summer, yellowed in autumn, pale and sparse in winter.

stats

Streak, walks & collection

Three boxes at the bottom of the welcome screen track your activity at a glance. All three dim when empty and brighten as you use the app.

  • 🔥 Streak — tap to see your current streak, best ever, and total days walked.
  • 👣 Walks — tap to see walk counts and time outside by week, month, year, and all time. Includes your longest walk.
  • 🎁 Found — tap to browse everything you've collected, sorted by rarity.
setup

API key

Tap ⚙ to add your Anthropic API key. It stays in your browser and is never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic when generating finds. A small credit goes a long way.

install

Install on Android

Open the app in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu, choose Add to Home Screen, then Install app. It launches full-screen with no browser bar, like a native app. Works with Minimalist Phone and similar launchers.
