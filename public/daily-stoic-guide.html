Field Guide

Daily Stoic

A private space to build resilience, cultivate gratitude, and reflect on what truly matters.

Core Practice

Daily Quote

Wisdom mapped to the calendar

Each day surfaces a new quote from Marcus Aurelius or Seneca. They are deterministically mapped to the day of the year (1-365), ensuring you'll receive the same sequence each year — providing a perfect anchor for annual reflection and observing your own growth against the same prompts.

Reflection

Seneca's Evening Interrogation

Before retiring for the night, use the built-in wizard based on Seneca's nightly review to reflect on your day. Ask yourself: What ailment or bad habit did I cure today? What failing did I resist? and In what matter can I show improvement tomorrow?

Stoic Exercises

Dichotomy of Control

Spheres of Choice

A dedicated interactive space to practice Epictetus's core teaching. Log your current worries or anxieties, and then sort them into what is "Up to Me" (your actions, choices, and judgments) and what is "Not Up to Me" (external events, other people's opinions). Watch the externals fade away as you consciously let them go.

Amor Fati

Love of Fate

When you face friction or setbacks, log them using the Amor Fati component. Tag the source of the friction, and reframe it not as an obstacle, but as raw material to practice patience, resilience, and acceptance.

Memento Mori

Life in Weeks

Remember that you will die. Enter your birth date to unlock a visualization of your life in weeks. Each filled square represents a week lived, providing a powerful visual reminder to value your remaining time.

Data & Settings

Reminders

Morning Prep & Evening Review

Configure custom push notifications directly from your browser. Set a time for your Morning Preparation (Premeditatio Malorum) and a separate time for your Evening Review. Notifications are scheduled locally using the Service Worker.

Privacy & Sync

Local by default

Your reflections are stored securely in your device's localStorage. There are no servers, no accounts, and no tracking. If you want cross-device sync, you can securely connect your own Notion database in the Settings panel.
