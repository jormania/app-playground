EXERCISE 01 — BACK & CORE

Kettlebell Swing

"This swings both ways — your spine says thanks, your ego might not survive."
  1. Stand feet shoulder-width, KB on floor between feet.
  2. Hinge at hips, grip with both hands, back neutral.
  3. Drive hips forward explosively — KB floats to shoulder height.
  4. Control the descent — pendulum back between legs.
  5. Hips do the work. If your arms burn, start over.
  6. Core braced, chest proud at the top of every rep.
Target: Back · Core · Glutes · Hamstrings
EXERCISE 02 — FULL BODY

Turkish Get-Up

"Half yoga. Half circus act. 100% regret in the morning."
  1. Lie flat, KB in right hand locked to ceiling. Eyes on the bell.
  2. Left palm flat; bend right knee, roll onto left elbow.
  3. Push to left hand, lift hips off the floor.
  4. Sweep left leg behind — come to a kneeling lunge.
  5. Stand fully upright, KB locked overhead throughout.
  6. Reverse every step exactly to return to floor. Same pace.
Target: Shoulders · Core · Stability · Everything
EXERCISE 03 — LEGS & CORE

Goblet Squat

"Drink from the chalice of gains. Your thighs will be smashed."
  1. Hold KB by the horns at chest height, elbows pointing down.
  2. Feet shoulder-width or wider, toes slightly out.
  3. Push knees out as you descend — tracking over toes.
  4. Go below parallel if mobility allows; chest stays tall.
  5. Drive through heels to stand, squeeze glutes at top.
  6. The KB is a counterweight — use it to stay upright.
Target: Quads · Glutes · Core · Mobility
EXERCISE 04 — BACK & ARMS

Single-Arm Row

"Row, row, row your gains. Gently down the stream of sweat."
  1. Left hand and knee on a bench, back flat and parallel.
  2. KB in right hand, arm hanging straight down.
  3. Brace core — no twisting, no wiggling, no excuses.
  4. Pull elbow up and back like starting a chainsaw.
  5. Squeeze shoulder blade hard at the top. Hold 1 second.
  6. Lower slowly — the descent is where gains live.
Target: Lats · Rhomboids · Biceps · Core
EXERCISE 05 — UPPER BODY

Clean & Press

"Clean conscience. Dirty muscles. Press on regardless."
  1. Hinge, swing KB up with a hip thrust — one arm.
  2. At shoulder height, rotate to rack: KB on forearm, fist at chin.
  3. From rack, press straight overhead — arm locks out fully.
  4. Core tight throughout. No banana back on the press.
  5. Lower to rack, swing back down in a controlled arc.
  6. One side per set — both sides deserve the suffering equally.
Target: Shoulders · Traps · Triceps · Core
EXERCISE 06 — CORE & MOBILITY

Windmill

"Tilting at windmills was never this painful. Thanks, Don Quixote."
  1. Press KB overhead with right arm locked. Eyes on the bell.
  2. Feet wide, toes angled left. Push right hip out to the right.
  3. Hinge sideways — left hand trails down left leg.
  4. Right arm stays locked. Left leg straight. Back flat.
  5. Drive through right hip to return upright.
  6. Be humble with the weight. Mobility wearing a strength costume.
Target: Obliques · Glutes · Shoulder Stability
EXERCISE 07 — POSTERIOR CHAIN

Romanian Deadlift

"Dracula never skipped deadlift day. Be as undying as your gains."
  1. Stand with KB(s), feet hip-width, soft bend in knees.
  2. Hinge at hips — push them straight back, not the waist.
  3. Let KB slide down legs as you descend, back stays neutral.
  4. Feel the hamstring stretch when KB reaches mid-shin.
  5. Drive hips forward to stand — squeeze glutes at top.
  6. The slow descent is where the hamstring magic happens.
Target: Hamstrings · Glutes · Lower Back
EXERCISE 08 — BACK & CORE

Renegade Row

"A plank. A row. A small sob. This is the way."
  1. Push-up position, both hands gripping KBs on the floor.
  2. Brace your entire body like a steel beam — rigid head to heels.
  3. Row one KB to hip, elbow tracking back. Core fights rotation.
  4. Lower it, repeat on the other side. Alternate every rep.
  5. Optional: add a push-up between rows for bonus suffering.
  6. Wider feet = easier to balance. Narrow feet = you enjoy pain.
Target: Back · Core · Shoulders · Stabilisers
EXERCISE 09 — SHOULDERS & CORE

Halo

"You are basically a saint. The halo is just the proof."
  1. Stand or kneel, hold KB upside-down by the bell at chest.
  2. Rotate KB around your head in one wide, smooth circle.
  3. Keep it tight to your skull — not a lazy orbit.
  4. Shoulders down and back. Core braced. No bowing at waist.
  5. Complete all reps in one direction, then reverse.
  6. Great for shoulder mobility and feeling oddly transcendent.
Target: Shoulders · Rotator Cuff · Mobility
EXERCISE 10 — CORE & GRIP

Figure-8

"Your hips don't lie. Your grip might give out first."
  1. Stand wide, soft bend in knees, hips hinged slightly forward.
  2. KB in right hand — pass it between left leg from the front.
  3. Left hand grabs behind left leg, passes under to the right.
  4. Right hand catches from front, between right leg.
  5. One continuous loop — try not to hit yourself (you will).
  6. Core tight, hips low, rhythm is the goal.
Target: Core · Grip · Coordination · Hip Flexors
EXERCISE 11 — FULL BODY

Farmer's Walk

"Be the farmer. Carry the weight. Question nothing."
  1. Stand between two KBs, deadlift setup, core tight.
  2. Pick them up — brace hard, neutral spine throughout.
  3. Walk forward with short, controlled steps. No waddling.
  4. Shoulders packed back and down — not shrugged up around ears.
  5. Walk a set distance or time, then set down with full control.
  6. Simple in concept. Brutal in execution. Welcome to real strength.
Target: Traps · Forearms · Core · Legs · Everything
EXERCISE 12 — POWER

High Pull

"Pull yourself up by the kettlebell handle. Growth is pain."
  1. Start with KB on floor — hinge and grip with one hand.
  2. Drive from hips — explosive thrust, same mechanics as swing.
  3. At shoulder height, pull elbow high and wide.
  4. KB floats briefly at shoulder level, elbow tops out above it.
  5. Control the descent back between legs with your hips.
  6. Explosive up, controlled down. Power without chaos.
Target: Traps · Upper Back · Shoulders · Power
