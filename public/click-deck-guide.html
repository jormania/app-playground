Click Deck — guide

A standalone web application for tracking graphic narrative and point-and-click adventure games, backed by your own live Notion database.

What it is

Click Deck is a specialized catalog for tracking your adventure game backlog, what you're currently playing, and what you've finished. It utilizes a retro-futuristic, cyberpunk aesthetic and synchronizes live with your own Notion database via our MCP proxy.

Everything is stored in your Notion. The app works on any device and installs as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

1. Quick Setup (Onboarding Wizard)

Click Deck features an automated Onboarding Wizard that will help you set up your database automatically in seconds. Here's how to run it:

  1. Create an Integration: Go to Notion Integrations and create a new integration. Call it "Click Deck".
  2. Copy the Token: Copy the Internal Integration Secret.
  3. Share a Page: Create a new blank page in your Notion workspace (e.g., "Gaming Setup"). In the top right corner of Notion, click `...`, go to Connections, and add your "Click Deck" integration to this page.
  4. Get the Page ID: Copy the link to this page. The long string of characters at the end of the URL is your Page ID.
  5. Run the Wizard: Open Click Deck, paste your Integration Token and the Page ID into the wizard, and click Initialize Database. Click Deck will automatically build the entire database schema for you!

2. Populating Seed Data

Once your database is initialized, you can start adding games manually, or you can populate it with our curated list of classics.

In the Settings (⚙) menu, click the Populate Seed Data button. This will automatically inject legendary games like Grim Fandango, The Secret of Monkey Island, and Disco Elysium straight into your Notion database, complete with high-resolution box art covers.

3. Usage & Features

  • Timeline View: Scroll through your games sorted chronologically by release year. The timeline features dynamic neon micro-animations as you scroll.
  • Analytics View: Advanced multi-dimensional filtering, Tag Word Cloud, and HTML Export functionality to slice and dice your collection.
  • Stats Screen: Deep dive into completion rates, top developer studios, and a financial breakdown of your collection's value.
  • Steam Pricing: A nightly automated cron job securely fetches real-time prices for your entire library based on the Steam App ID.
  • Administration Scripts: You can find maintenance scripts in the scripts/ directory to manually force-sync prices or verify any incorrectly tagged Steam App IDs.
  • Random Game: Can't decide what to play? Click the [R]ANDOM button to pull a random game from your Backlog!
  • Themes: Switch between three distinct aesthetics in the Settings menu (Union City, Voodoo, and Noir) to match your current vibe.
